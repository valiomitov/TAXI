Frequently asked questions

Answers to the most common questions about our Burgas airport transfers.

Where will the driver wait at Burgas Airport?

Your driver waits in the arrivals hall right after customs with a sign showing your name. Your confirmation email also includes a direct phone number.

Do I pay extra if my flight is delayed?

No. We track flight schedules in real time. If your flight is delayed the driver is notified automatically and waits at no extra charge.

How long is the transfer from the airport to Sunny Beach?

Around 25–30 minutes. Nessebar is about 20 minutes and Sveti Vlas about 35 minutes, depending on traffic.

Does the price include airport parking and tolls?

Yes. Parking, toll roads and fuel are all included in the fixed price — you pay nothing beyond the quoted amount.

What are the payment options?

Pay cash in euro directly to the driver on arrival, prepay by bank transfer, or pay online by card.

Do I need to book in advance?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours ahead, especially in summer. For last-minute transfers, contact us directly via WhatsApp/Viber.
